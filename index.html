Our Story

Adornment by Ade is born from a reverence for Yoruba heritage and the craft of goldsmithing. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted in small batches using ethically sourced 18k gold. The brand honors village artisans, passing down techniques through generations, merging tradition with contemporary luxury.

  • Traditional Yoruba motifs reimagined in modern forms
  • Sustainably sourced materials and responsible practices
  • Showcasing Nigerian artisans on the global stage
